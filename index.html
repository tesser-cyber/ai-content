Laos Deposit Process (ฝากเงินลาว)

This section explains how to deposit in Laos for users using LAK (Kip).

How to deposit in Laos?

  1. Click Deposit.
  2. Select the account you wish to deposit into.
  3. Enter the deposit amount.
Note:
  • The minimum deposit amount is $50.
  • The system will automatically convert and display the amount in LAK (Kip).

After completing the transfer

Please provide the following details in the system:

  • Sender’s bank account number.
  • Transfer date (based on the payment slip).
  • Upload the transfer slip.
Important: The bank account number and transfer date must match the payment slip. Otherwise, the deposit may be delayed or rejected.

Forex/Gold Margin Calculation [cite: 943]

Margin = (Lot * Contract Size / Leverage) * Exchange Rate [cite: 945]

Pip Value Formula [cite: 865]

Pip Value = ((Contract Size * Lot) * 10^-digits) / Exchange Rate [cite: 865, 1095]

Swap Rate Formula [cite: 1094]

Swap = Pip Value * Swap Rate * Number of Holding Days [cite: 1094]

Crypto Spread Calculation [cite: 914]

Spread Cost = [Spread * (Contract Size * 10^-digit * Lot)] * Account Currency Rate [cite: 917]
